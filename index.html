Research Statement

    I am interested in effectively combining visual intelligence with robotics. I regard vision as a cornerstone for understanding and interacting with our environment. We live in a dynamic 4D world, constantly seeing, understanding, and interacting with objects and environments. How can we perceive and understand a complex 4D world from video, like humans do? Or even more, how can we make decisions based on it? By enabling robots to observe, understand, and interact with their environment, my research aims to bridge the gap between perception and action, bringing machines closer to seamless integration with the world. Currently, my research focuses on the first half—visual perception.
